Let's redesign WebSTAC

We love WebSTAC, but let's admit it - its UI is a bit dated. Time for a refresh! Join us at the WebSTAC Designathon and show off your design skills to give WebSTAC a modern makeover. Let's bring WebSTAC into the 21st century - can't wait to see your creative ideas in action!

Event details

The WebSTAC Designathon will take place at the Skandalaris Center, Mallinckrodt 128, on Friday, April 21, 2023, from 6:00 pm to 8:30 pm. We'll provide a relaxed and collaborative environment, with ample tables and chairs for teamwork. We'll also have free snacks and stickers!

Organized by DSC WashU and the Skandalaris Center

The WebSTAC Designathon is brought to you by DSC WashU, a computer science-focused student group at WashU, and the Skandalaris Center, an initiative that supports WashU entrepreneurship.
