Let's redesign WebSTAC

If you've ever looked at WebSTAC and thought, "man, this looks straight out of the 90s", join us at the WebSTAC Designathon, and apply your creativity to prototype a redesign! The best redesign will win $500.

Event details

The WebSTAC Designathon will take place at the Skandalaris Center, Mallinckrodt 128, on Friday, April 21, 2023, from 6:00 pm to 8:30 pm. We'll provide ample tables and chairs for teamwork, plus free snacks and stickers!

Organized by DSC WashU and the Skandalaris Center

The WebSTAC Designathon is brought to you by DSC WashU, a computer science-focused student group, and the Skandalaris Center, the home of WashU entrepreneurship.
